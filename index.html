Why Berlin

A city that welcomes
the newly arrived.

Berlin has always drawn creatives from everywhere. But arriving in a new city, even a welcoming one, can feel isolating. Carte Berlin exists for the in-between period: after you land, before you've found your people.

We rotate venues every month across studios, cafés, and coworking spaces. The location changes. The card stays the same. The question on it is always yours.

First Thursday of every month, 7 to 10 PM
Rotating venues across Mitte, Kreuzberg & Prenzlauer Berg
Free for newcomers (under 6 months in Berlin)
