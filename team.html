The people behind it

Meet Our Team

Six people. Six countries. Everyone arrived in Berlin and found something missing. We built what we wished had existed. Every team member still wears a card.

Sofia Reyes

🇲🇽Mexico
Co-founder & Event Director

Sofia moved to Berlin in 2021 and spent her first three months feeling like a tourist. She started Loop in 2022 with the belief that one printed card could change that.

Felix Brandt

🇩🇪Germany
Co-founder & Operations

Felix has been running creative events in Berlin since 2018. He handles venues, logistics, and making sure the coffee is actually good.

Aisha Nkomo

🇳🇬Nigeria
Community Lead

Aisha joined as a community member in month two and never left. She now leads outreach, partnerships, and welcomes every newcomer at the door.

Takeshi Mori

🇯🇵Japan
Visual Identity

Takeshi designed the original card format, the one that stayed. He's responsible for every printed, stamped, and folded thing Loop puts in your hands.

Marta Kowalska

🇵🇱Poland
Digital & Web

Marta keeps everything online: the site, the registration, the newsletter. She believes the digital layer should disappear so the physical one can shine.

Lena Osei

🇬🇭Ghana
Photography & Documentation

Lena shows up to every event with one camera and a commitment to capturing the moments between moments: the card reads, the shy first hellos.
